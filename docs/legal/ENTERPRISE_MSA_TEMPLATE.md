**Between**: Sora2 ("Provider")

## 1. DEFINITIONS & INTERPRETATION

### 1.1 Definitions

- **"Enterprise Services"** means the AI content generation, video generation, Batch API, and related services provided by Sora2 under this Agreement.

- **"SEO Infrastructure"** means the index health monitoring, sitemap integrity validation, automated scaling controls, and risk alert systems provided as part of Enterprise Services.

- **"Index Health Metrics"** means the measurements of Discovered, Crawled, and Indexed URLs, Index Rate, and related SEO performance indicators tracked by Sora2's systems.

- **"Scaling Activities"** means the generation, publication, or expansion of AI-generated content intended for search engine indexing.

- **"Credits"** means the prepaid service units tracked in Customer's credit_wallet account, representing access to Enterprise Services.

- **"Scaling Gate"** means the automated system that controls whether Scaling Activities may proceed based on Index Health Metrics.

### 1.2 Interpretation

References to "including" mean "including without limitation." Headings are for convenience only and do not affect interpretation.

---

## 2. SCOPE OF SERVICES

### 2.1 Services Provided

Provider agrees to provide Customer with:

(a) Enterprise Batch API access for content and video generation
(b) SEO Infrastructure and Index Health monitoring
(c) Dashboard access for visibility into service metrics
(d) Technical support as specified in the applicable Service Level Agreement

### 2.2 Services Expressly Excluded

The following are NOT included in Enterprise Services:

(a) Guaranteed search engine indexing, ranking, or visibility
(b) Manual SEO optimization, link building, or content strategy services
(c) Traffic, revenue, or business outcome guarantees
(d) Circumvention of Scaling Gate controls

### 2.3 Acknowledgement

Customer acknowledges that search engine behavior is determined by third parties and is outside Provider's control.

---

## 3. SERVICE DELIVERY & ACCESS

### 3.1 Access Provision

Provider will provide Customer with:
- API credentials and documentation
- Dashboard access for authorized users
- Technical integration support during onboarding

### 3.2 Customer Responsibilities

Customer agrees to:
- Provide accurate account and billing information
- Maintain appropriate security for API credentials
- Use services only for lawful purposes
- Not circumvent or disable SEO safety mechanisms

### 3.3 Prohibited Activities

Customer shall not:
- Attempt to bypass Scaling Gate controls
- Use services for spam, black-hat SEO, or deceptive practices
- Resell services without written authorization
- Exceed usage limits specified in the Order Form

---

## 4. SEO & INDEXING RISK ALLOCATION

### 4.1 No Indexing Guarantee

Customer acknowledges that search engine indexing, ranking, and visibility are determined solely by third-party search engines. Provider does not guarantee that any page, content, or URL will be indexed, ranked, or maintained in search results.

### 4.2 SEO Infrastructure Commitment

Provider commits to providing enterprise-grade SEO Infrastructure including:
- Daily Index Health Metrics monitoring
- Sitemap integrity validation
- Automated Scaling Gate controls
- Risk alerts and operational safeguards

### 4.3 Automatic Scaling Control

Provider reserves the right to automatically suspend or limit Scaling Activities when Index Health Metrics indicate elevated risk, including but not limited to:
- Index Rate falling below 40%
- Detection of empty sitemap chunks
- Sustained zero or negative index growth

Such suspension shall not constitute breach of this Agreement.

### 4.4 Risk Isolation

SEO-related controls shall not affect availability of:
- Batch API services
- Credit-based usage and billing
- Video generation services
- Non-SEO content generation

---

## 5. FEES, BILLING & CREDITS

### 5.1 Fees

Customer agrees to pay fees as specified in the Order Form. Enterprise Services are provided on a fixed-fee basis (monthly or annual).

### 5.2 Credits

Credits represent access to Enterprise Services and are:
- Non-refundable except as required by law
- Not transferable to other accounts
- Subject to expiration as specified in Order Form

### 5.3 Pricing Philosophy

Provider does not use usage-based pricing for SEO-related services. Fixed pricing reflects risk ownership, not volume consumption.

### 5.4 Payment Terms

Invoices are due within thirty (30) days of receipt. Late payments may incur interest at 1.5% per month.

## 8. COMPLIANCE & ACCEPTABLE USE

### 8.1 Legal Compliance

Customer agrees to use services in compliance with all applicable laws and regulations.

### 8.2 Content Standards

Customer shall not use services to generate:

## 9. LIMITATION OF LIABILITY

### 9.1 Liability Cap

Provider's total liability under this Agreement shall not exceed the fees paid by Customer in the twelve (12) months preceding the claim.

### 9.2 Exclusions

Provider shall not be liable for:
- Loss of search traffic, rankings, or visibility
- Loss of revenue or business opportunities
- Indirect, incidental, or consequential damages
- Damages arising from search engine algorithm changes
- Damages arising from Customer's failure to follow Provider guidance

### 9.3 Essential Purpose

These limitations apply regardless of whether the limited remedy fails of its essential purpose.

---

## 10. TERM & TERMINATION

### 10.1 Term

This Agreement commences on the Effective Date and continues for the Initial Term specified in the Order Form, thereafter renewing automatically for successive periods unless terminated.

### 10.2 Termination for Convenience

Either party may terminate by providing ninety (90) days written notice before the end of the then-current term.

### 10.3 Termination for Cause

Either party may terminate immediately upon material breach that remains uncured for thirty (30) days after written notice.

### 10.4 Effect of Termination

Upon termination:
- Customer's access to services will cease
- Outstanding fees become immediately due
- Confidentiality obligations survive

### 10.5 Scaling Control Not a Breach

Automatic suspension of Scaling Activities due to SEO risk controls does not constitute breach or grounds for termination by Customer.
