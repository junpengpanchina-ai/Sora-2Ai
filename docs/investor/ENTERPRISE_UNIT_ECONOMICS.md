## Pricing Logic

### Core Principle

```
We don't price by usage. We price by risk ownership.

Enterprise customers pay for safe scaling, not generation volume.
```

### Why This Matters

| Traditional AI Tools | Sora2 Enterprise |
|---------------------|------------------|
| Price by tokens/pages | Price by risk control |
| Usage-based, volatile | Contract-based, predictable |
| Customer owns SEO risk | Sora2 manages SEO risk |

## Retention & Expansion

### High Retention Drivers

- SEO Infra deeply embedded in customer workflow
- Operational lock-in (dashboard, alerts, SOP)
- Switching cost is high once scaled

### Expansion Vectors

- Higher page volume (10k → 100k)
- Additional regions / languages
- Upgrade from Core → Scale

## Pricing Philosophy（内部参考）

### 为什么不能卖便宜

1. SEO Infra 是真实成本（监控、存储、告警）
2. 客户 SEO 事故 = 你的声誉风险
3. 便宜 = 客户不重视 = 用坏了怪你

### 为什么不按页数定价

1. 页数多 ≠ 价值高
2. 真正的价值是"规模化风险控制"
3. 按页数定价会鼓励客户"堆量"而不是"堆质量"

### 定价锚点话术

```
Anyone can generate 100,000 pages.
We make sure you don't regret it.
```
